About

India Geodata is a unified repository of India's openly-licensed geospatial data. It consolidates datasets from multiple community projects and government portals into a single, well-organized collection.

Why this exists

India's geospatial data is scattered across dozens of government portals, community repositories, and research projects. Finding the right boundary file means searching through multiple sources, dealing with inconsistent formats, and navigating different licensing terms. This repository solves that by bringing everything into one place with consistent documentation and clear download paths.
